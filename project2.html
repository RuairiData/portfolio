project 2 — Reporting / MI Dashboard

Problem: Stakeholders needed a single KPI view and a reliable way to explain performance drivers.

Data: Public/synthetic dataset at monthly grain (policies/transactions/claims).

Approach:

  • Modelled a star schema and defined KPI measures consistently.
  • Built drilldowns for variance/drivers and segmented performance.
  • Added basic QA checks (missing keys, duplicates, outliers).

Outcome: Clear executive view + driver analysis; reduced manual reconciliation and improved confidence in the numbers.
